Are there any VR headsets that dont use cords?

Yes. There are many VR headsets that don't use cords. If you buy one without a cord you don't have to worry about tripping over it. Make sure you do research beforehand so you know witch brands do and don't have cords.

How lond can I where the headset without straining my eyes?

It depends for some people, but the average time you should spend playing on a VR headset is around 30 minutes, and make sure to take around 10 to 15-minute breaks so your body can recover.

There are not many reports about this info but VR is safe to use as long as you are in a big space where you won't run into anything that could make you trip or fall. Also if your VR headset comes with a cord make sure it is safely out of the way.
